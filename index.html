Parul University

Knowledge. Innovation. Excellence.

Parul University, located in Vadodara, Gujarat, is one of India's leading private universities. It offers a wide range of diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as Engineering, Management, Law, Medicine, Pharmacy, Design, Arts, and more.

Established with a vision to provide holistic education, the university is home to students from across India and over 70 different countries, making it a truly global campus. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, research opportunities, industry collaborations, and vibrant campus life, Parul University continues to shape the leaders of tomorrow.
